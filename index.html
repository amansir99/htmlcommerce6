Frequently Asked Questions

Do you authenticate rare books and first editions?

Yes, all rare books and first editions undergo rigorous authentication by our team of bibliographic experts before being offered for sale.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all books in original condition. Rare and signed editions have special handling procedures detailed in your order confirmation.

Do you offer personalized reading recommendations?

Absolutely! Our literary experts provide personalized recommendations based on your reading history and preferences. Simply contact our concierge service.

How do you ensure books arrive in perfect condition?

Each book is carefully inspected, wrapped in archival materials, and shipped in protective packaging designed specifically for books and rare manuscripts.

Do you have a membership or loyalty program?

Yes, our Literary Circle membership offers exclusive discounts, early access to rare editions, and invitations to author events and literary discussions.
